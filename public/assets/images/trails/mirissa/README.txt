# Mirissa Beach Images

Place 4 high-quality images in this directory:

## Required Images:
1. **main.jpg** - Mirissa beach panoramic view
   - Beautiful crescent-shaped beach with palm trees
   - Golden sand and clear blue waters

2. **gallery-1.jpg** - Whale watching experience
   - Boat on ocean during whale watching tour
   - Or actual whale sighting moment

3. **gallery-2.jpg** - Beach sunset/sunrise
   - Stunning sunset or sunrise over the ocean
   - Silhouettes of palm trees or boats

4. **gallery-3.jpg** - Coastal lifestyle
   - Local fishermen, beach activities, or coastal village
   - Shows the authentic Mirissa experience

## Image Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Main image**: 1200x800px recommended
- **Gallery images**: 800x600px recommended
- **File size**: Max 500KB per image
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, sharp focus
