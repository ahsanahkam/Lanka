# Ella Rock Images

Place 4 high-quality images in this directory:

## Required Images:
1. **main.jpg** - Ella Rock hiking trail
   - The iconic Ella Rock formation
   - Shows the hiking path and scenic beauty

2. **gallery-1.jpg** - Tea plantation views
   - Rolling green tea estates during the hike
   - Workers plucking tea leaves or tea factory views

3. **gallery-2.jpg** - Summit panorama
   - 360-degree view from Ella Rock summit
   - Shows Ella Gap and surrounding mountains

4. **gallery-3.jpg** - Train journey/Nine Arch Bridge
   - Famous Nine Arch Bridge near Ella
   - Or the scenic blue train journey through hill country

## Image Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Main image**: 1200x800px recommended
- **Gallery images**: 800x600px recommended
- **File size**: Max 500KB per image
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, sharp focus
