# Yala National Park Images

Place 4 high-quality images in this directory:

## Required Images:
1. **main.jpg** - Yala park landscape
   - Wide shot of the park's diverse ecosystem
   - Savanna, scrubland, or coastal areas within the park

2. **gallery-1.jpg** - Leopard or big cats
   - Sri Lankan leopard (park's main attraction)
   - Or other wild cats like fishing cats

3. **gallery-2.jpg** - Elephants
   - Wild elephants in their natural habitat
   - Family groups or individual magnificent specimens

4. **gallery-3.jpg** - Bird watching/biodiversity
   - Exotic birds (peacocks, eagles, hornbills)
   - Or diverse wildlife like sloth bears, deer, crocodiles

## Image Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Main image**: 1200x800px recommended
- **Gallery images**: 800x600px recommended
- **File size**: Max 500KB per image
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, sharp focus
