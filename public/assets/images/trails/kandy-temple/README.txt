# Kandy Temple (Sacred Tooth Relic) Images

Place 4 high-quality images in this directory:

## Required Images:
1. **main.jpg** - Temple exterior
   - Beautiful temple architecture with golden roofs
   - Lake Kandy and temple complex view

2. **gallery-1.jpg** - Interior/shrine
   - Sacred tooth relic shrine room
   - Ornate interior decorations and Buddhist art

3. **gallery-2.jpg** - Religious ceremonies
   - Daily puja ceremonies with monks
   - Devotees offering prayers and flowers

4. **gallery-3.jpg** - Temple complex
   - Museum, other buildings in the complex
   - Or traditional Kandyan architecture details

## Image Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Main image**: 1200x800px recommended
- **Gallery images**: 800x600px recommended
- **File size**: Max 500KB per image
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, sharp focus
